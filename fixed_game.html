Pixel Pizza Quest - Fixed Version

Controls:

  • Left/Right Arrow Keys: Move left/right
  • Up Arrow Key: Jump (can combine with left/right for diagonal jumps)
  • Spacebar: Shoot projectiles (only one at a time; wait for it to hit something)

Note: You can only have one projectile active at a time. Watch the indicator in the top-right corner to know when you can shoot again.

Open your browser's developer console (F12 or Cmd+Option+I) to see debug messages
